What is biometrics?

The shelf life of prints is 5 years. This means that by taking this picture once, you can forget about this formality for the coming years. At each subsequent visa request, your data will be retrieved from the VIS;

- Do not take this procedure can only children under the age of five years. Those whose age is already 5-15 years old must come to fingerprinting with their parents.

You should know that no fee is charged for taking your fingerprints. A special scan is taken, so the whole process takes no more than 5 minutes.

2015 was a turning point in the issue of improving the migration rules of Europe.

It was in 2015 that the rule came into force, which states that everyone who applies for a Schengen visa must be fingerprinted, regardless of which sample passport they own.

The new law states:

- This procedure is necessary for all those who apply for a visa for the first time since the entry into force of the innovation;

- Those with a Schengen stamp still valid can cross the EU border without following this prescription;
